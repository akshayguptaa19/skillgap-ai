Skill Extraction Logic (Hybrid Model)

1

Text Normalization

Raw text is pre-processed to remove noise, normalize case, and handle special characters, ensuring accurate matching of tech terms like .Net, C++, and Node.js.

2

Hybrid Extraction Pipeline

The engine combines three layers of detection:

  • Keyword Match: Fast, accurate regex-based lookup for 250+ standard skills.
  • SpaCy NER: Uses Linguistic features to identify entities in the resume.
  • BERT Semantics: Uses SBERT (Sentence-BERT) to understand the *context* and extract skills that keyword matching might miss.

3

AI Optimization

Beyond just gap analysis, the system identifies missing skills from the JD and suggests how to naturally incorporate them into the "Optimized Resume" preview.
